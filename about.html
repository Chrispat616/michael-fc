ABOUT US

Learn about our journey, mission and vision

Our Story

Michael FC began as a small community club with big dreams. What started with just few passionate players has grown into one of the most respected football clubs in the region. Our journey has been marked by dedication, perseverance, and an unwavering commitment to excellence both on and off the field.

Our Mission

To develop exceptional football talent while fostering character, teamwork, and sportsmanship. We are committed to providing world- class training facilities and coaching that enables our players to reach their full potential, both as athletes and as individuals who contribute positively to their communities.

Our Vision

To become the football club that sets the standard for excellence in youth development and competitive play. We envision a future where Thunder FC is recognized globally for producing not just skilled players, but well- rounded individuals who embody the values of integrity, respect, and determination.
